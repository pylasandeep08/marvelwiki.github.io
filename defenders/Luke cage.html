Luke Cage

Synopsis

A man with super strength protects his neighborhood.

Plot

After a sabotaged experiment leaves him with superhuman strength and unbreakable skin, Luke Cage becomes a fugitive trying to rebuild his life in Harlem. However, he is forced to step out of the shadows and fight crime, protecting his community from powerful enemies like Cottonmouth and Mariah Dillard.
