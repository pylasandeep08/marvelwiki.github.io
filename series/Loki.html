Loki (Season 1 & 2)

Synopsis

Loki explores time, multiverse, and his true purpose across two seasons.

Plot

After escaping with the Tesseract, Loki is captured by the Time Variance Authority (TVA). He is forced to help track dangerous timeline variants, including Sylvie. In Season 1, Loki discovers the truth behind the Sacred Timeline and meets He Who Remains, the creator of the TVA. Sylvie kills him, breaking the timeline and unleashing the multiverse. In Season 2, Loki struggles with time slipping and works with Mobius and the TVA to fix the collapsing timelines. He learns to control time and ultimately sacrifices himself, becoming the guardian of all timelines, holding the multiverse together.
