Guardians of the Galaxy (2014)

Synopsis

A group of misfits team up to protect a powerful Infinity Stone from the villain Ronan.

Plot

Peter Quill forms a team with Gamora, Drax, Rocket, and Groot. Together they stop Ronan from using the Power Stone, becoming the Guardians of the Galaxy.
