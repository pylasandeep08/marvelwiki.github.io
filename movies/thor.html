Thor (2011)

Synopsis

Thor, the God of Thunder, is banished to Earth and stripped of his powers. He must learn humility to become worthy again.

Plot

Thor is cast out by Odin and lands on Earth. Meanwhile, Loki plots to take control of Asgard. Thor regains his power after proving his worth and defeats Loki’s plan, saving both Earth and Asgard.
