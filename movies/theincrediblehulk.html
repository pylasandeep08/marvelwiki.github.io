Synopsis

Scientist Bruce Banner is exposed to gamma radiation, turning him into the Hulk whenever he becomes angry. While hiding from the military, he searches for a cure but must face a dangerous enemy known as the Abomination.

Plot

Bruce Banner lives in hiding while searching for a cure to his condition. General Ross relentlessly hunts him. Meanwhile, soldier Emil Blonsky volunteers for an experiment that turns him into the powerful Abomination. Banner transforms into Hulk and battles Blonsky in a destructive final fight, eventually defeating him.
