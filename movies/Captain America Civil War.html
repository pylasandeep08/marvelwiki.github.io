Captain America: Civil War (2016)

Synopsis

The Avengers split into two teams over government control.

Plot

After collateral damage incidents, the Sokovia Accords divide the Avengers. Iron Man supports regulation, while Captain America opposes it. This leads to a massive battle. The truth about Bucky killing Tony’s parents breaks the team apart.
