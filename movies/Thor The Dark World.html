Thor: The Dark World (2013)

Synopsis

Thor teams up with Loki to stop the Dark Elf Malekith from destroying the universe.

Plot

Malekith seeks the Aether to bring darkness to all realms. Thor joins forces with Loki to stop him. After a fierce battle, Thor defeats Malekith but loses Loki—though Loki secretly survives.
