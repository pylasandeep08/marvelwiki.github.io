Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014)

Synopsis

Captain America faces a deadly assassin known as the Winter Soldier while uncovering a conspiracy within S.H.I.E.L.D.

Plot

Steve Rogers works with Black Widow and Falcon to uncover Hydra’s infiltration of S.H.I.E.L.D. The Winter Soldier is revealed to be Bucky Barnes, Steve’s friend. Steve saves Bucky and destroys Hydra’s plan.
