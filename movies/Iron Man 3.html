Iron Man 3 (2013)

Synopsis

Tony Stark faces a powerful terrorist called the Mandarin while struggling with PTSD after the Avengers battle.

Plot

Tony Stark suffers anxiety attacks and builds multiple Iron Man suits. He investigates bombings linked to the Mandarin, only to discover the real villain is Aldrich Killian. Using his intelligence, Stark defeats Killian and destroys his suits, choosing a simpler life.
